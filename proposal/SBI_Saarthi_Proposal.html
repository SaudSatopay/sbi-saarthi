01 / THE PROBLEM

Engagement that waits,
guesses, and offloads the work.

Digital engagement today is reactive (it waits for you to open YONO), generic (one offer blasted to everyone) and unactionable (it tells you something, then asks you to do it). At SBI's scale, that is hundreds of crores of relationships reduced to a notification nobody opens.

Reactive
most customers never go beyond a balance check · the bank never reaches out first
Generic
broadcast SMS & push → notification blindness, low CTR, opt-outs
Unactionable
advice without execution · the customer abandons before completing
1 advisor for ~1 in 100

A relationship manager exists for roughly the top 1%. The other 49.5 crore customers get the same broadcast SMS. Engagement isn't a data problem — it's an attention-and-action problem: the right nudge, at the right moment, finished for them.

02 / WHY NOW

The engagement layer
nobody has built.

FORCE 01

Agentic AI crossed the line

Reliable tool-use and multi-agent orchestration (LangGraph, Claude) now let software act safely on a customer's behalf — not just chat. Autonomy with guardrails is finally production-grade.

From chatbots to agents that act
FORCE 02

Bharat is consent-ready

UPI-scale rails, the live Account Aggregator consent framework, and Bhashini's 22-language stack make vernacular, consented, real-time action possible at population scale for the first time.

Consent · vernacular · real-time
FORCE 03

The churn clock is ticking

Fintechs already own daily engagement; idle CASA and dormant accounts are lifetime value bleeding out. Proactive, personal engagement is now a balance-sheet defence, not a nicety.

Engagement defends the balance sheet
The unfilled gap

Banks can detect moments (analytics) and have the channels (YONO, WhatsApp) — but the proactive, explainable, action-closing, vernacular engagement layer that reaches the right customer, completes the action under consent and guardrails, and learns from every outcome does not exist. That is Saarthi: an agent crew that turns a detected signal into a finished, audited action.

Model mix — right model for each job
▸ Claude — planning, tool-use, guardrail reasoning
▸ Fine-tuned SLMs — high-volume routine NBA
▸ XGBoost / LightGBM — propensity scoring
▸ Contextual bandit — timing & channel choice
▸ Embeddings + RAG — product / policy retrieval
▸ Bhashini / Sarvam — 22-language ASR · TTS · MT
Cost-tiered: small models handle ~90% of routine actions; Claude is reserved for complex, high-stakes reasoning.

10 / TRUST & SECURITY

Bank-grade trust,
by design.

Consent
Account Aggregator (Sahamati) — tokenised, purpose-limited, revocable.
Privacy
DPDP Act 2023 — PII redaction & tokenisation, data minimisation, India residency.
Regulatory
RBI digital-lending & outsourcing norms; SEBI suitability for any MF advice.
AI guardrails
OPA policy engine + LLM-as-judge; suitability, frequency caps, allow/deny actions.
Security
Encryption in transit & at rest, Vault/HSM secrets, RBAC, prompt-injection defence.
Auditability
Immutable audit trail, full agent trace, explainable decisions, human override.
