SBI Saarthi
Your bank, one step ahead.
A bank waits for you to open the app, blasts the same offer to everyone, then leaves you to do the work. For SBI's 50 crore customers, a relationship manager exists only for the wealthiest 1%. Saarthi turns engagement into a crew of autonomous AI agents that sense each customer's money-moment, reach them in their own language, and complete the action for them — book the FD, shift the EMI, revive the account — every step consent-gated, guardrail-checked and audit-logged.

01
The Problem

Engagement that waits,
guesses, and offloads the work.

Digital engagement today is reactive, generic and unactionable. At SBI's scale, that's hundreds of crores of relationships reduced to a notification nobody opens.

Reactive
It waits for the customer to open YONO. Most never go beyond a balance check — the bank never reaches out first.
Generic
Broadcast SMS & push to everyone — notification blindness, collapsing CTR, opt-outs.
Unactionable
It gives advice, then asks the customer to do the work. They abandon before completing.
A relationship manager exists for ~1% of customers. The other 49.5 crore get the same broadcast SMS — engagement is an attention-and-action problem, not a data one.

The unfilled gap

Model mix — right model for each job
▸ Claude — planning, tool-use, guardrails
▸ Fine-tuned SLMs — high-volume routine NBA
▸ XGBoost / LightGBM — propensity scoring
▸ Contextual bandit — timing & channel
▸ Embeddings + RAG — product / policy
▸ Bhashini / Sarvam — 22-language ASR·TTS·MT
Cost-tiered: small models handle ~90% of routine actions; Claude is reserved for complex, high-stakes reasoning.

Trust & Security

Bank-grade trust,
by design.

Consent
Account Aggregator (Sahamati) — tokenised, purpose-limited, revocable.
Privacy
DPDP Act 2023 — PII redaction & tokenisation, data minimisation, India residency.
Regulatory
RBI digital-lending & outsourcing norms; SEBI suitability for any MF advice.
AI guardrails
OPA policy engine + LLM-as-judge; suitability, frequency caps, allow/deny actions.
Security
Encryption in transit & at rest, Vault/HSM secrets, RBAC, prompt-injection defence.
Auditability
Immutable audit trail, full agent trace, explainable decisions, human override.

Saarthi makes every one of SBI's 50 crore customers feel personally banked — proactively, in their own language, with the action already done. That is Digital Engagement, finished.
